About Manna Study Centre

Leading the way in personalized education and childcare for over 10 years.

Founded on Trust

Manna Study Centre was founded by a team of passionate educators who realized that parents needed a reliable destination for both academic excellence and supervised care.

We started with a small batch of 5 students and have grown into a community of hundreds of families. Our mission: to treat every child as our own.
